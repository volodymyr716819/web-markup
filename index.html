Our lessons

You want to learn how to surf or you are dreaming to try kitesurfing, but you do not know where to start? Our friendly instructors will happily help you to catch the first wave! You can get to know more about our lessons on our Lessons page.

Rental

If you are new in surfing or kitesurfing, most probably you do not have your own equipment to start. We are happy to help you with this! We propose wide range of boards, wetsuits and other stuff which will help you to enjoy ocean. To get to know more, visit our Rental page.

Opening hours

We want to be sure that you will not miss the best wave of the day! That's why we are open seven days a week and even more on your request. To see our opening times, check out our Opening Hours page.

Contact Us
