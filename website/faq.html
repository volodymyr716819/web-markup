I am 9 years old, is it too early to start surfing?

Of course not! Surfing is for everyone, no matter how old or young you are. You just need to be brave and ready to have fun!

What do I need to bring for a surf lesson?

You should pack your swimsuit, a towel and sunscreen. The surf school will give you everything else you need, like a surfboard, a wetsuit and some awesome tips.

How do I sign up for a surf lesson?

It's super easy! You can book a lesson at our school or by contacting us via phone or email.

Is surfing safe?

Yes, surfing is safe if you follow some simple rules and respect the ocean and other surfers. Our instructors will always keep an eye on you and help you if you need it.

How many lessons should I take to surf/kite like a pro?

It depends on you. The main aim of our lessons is to make you feeling safe and having fun. Usually for surfing it is enough to take 1-3 lessons to go further on your own. For kiting we would recommend to take lessons until you feel confident and independent with the kite. But anyway, never go kiting alone, always make sure somebody is around.

Which wetsuit/surfboard/kite should I buy?

Our instructors will be happy to help you to choose the right equipment and will recommend shops nearby. Moreover you can try it during your lesson.

Surfing is summer kind of sport, right?

Absolutely not! Advanced surfers doing it all year round. But to try it you should have appropriate wetsuit.

What is the best board for surfing?

There is no such thing! There are a lot of types/shapes/colors/producers of surfboards. Usually surfers have several boards in their collection. You will find your favorite when you start surfing.

Can animals surf?

Yes! You can find a lot exciting videos on YouTube.

When the earliest you can start teaching a kid how to surf?

As soon as parents want it! When parents are surfers they share their hobbies with their kids from the very beginning. You can find many successful examples on social media.

Why is the school named Surfing Cat?

Good question! :) The thing is we have a cat who became the symbol of our school. He loves sea as much as we do! And we are welcoming you to meet him!
